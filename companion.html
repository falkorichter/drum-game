ℹ️ How It Works

1. Run npm run wireless on the game computer.
2. Open the URL it prints (e.g. http://192.168.x.x:8765/companion) here on your phone.
3. Enter the ws:// address in the Connection box above and tap Connect.
4. Select your player role (P1 or P2).
5. Enable motion detection — or just tap the HIT button.
6. When the game starts and the beat approaches, strike the phone like a drum stick! 🥁
